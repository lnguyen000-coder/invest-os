# Shopify Inc. (SHOP.TO) — Investment Thesis



## Thesis in one paragraph
Shopify is building the operating system for global commerce by enabling businesses of all sizes to start, manage, and grow across online, offline, and international sales channels. I believe the market often focuses on near-term e-commerce growth rates while underestimating Shopify's ability to expand its ecosystem through payments, fulfillment partnerships, financial services, AI tools, and enterprise merchants. As more commerce shifts toward independent brands and omnichannel selling, Shopify has the opportunity to increase revenue per merchant while strengthening its competitive position. I believe Shopify can compound intrinsic value over the next decade through ecosystem expansion, operating leverage, and disciplined innovation.

## Moat
Merchant ecosystem

Shopify is much more than an online storefront. Merchants rely on payments, checkout, inventory, shipping integrations, marketing, analytics, POS, financing, and thousands of third-party applications.

Switching costs

As merchants grow, migrating away from Shopify becomes increasingly expensive due to integrations, customer data, workflows, themes, apps, and operational complexity.

Developer ecosystem

A large developer and partner community continuously expands Shopify's capabilities, making the platform more valuable over time.

Brand

For entrepreneurs and growing businesses, Shopify has become synonymous with starting and scaling an independent online business.

Scale

Millions of merchants generate network effects that attract more developers, agencies, payment providers, and technology partners.

## What I'm paying for growth
I am comfortable paying a premium if:

GMV continues growing sustainably.
Merchant Solutions become a larger share of revenue.
Shopify Payments penetration increases.
Free cash flow grows faster than revenue.
Enterprise adoption continues expanding.

I become cautious if:

Merchant growth slows materially.
GMV growth decelerates for structural reasons.
Merchant retention weakens.
Operating leverage disappears.
Valuation becomes disconnected from long-term cash generation.

## Kill criteria (what would make me sell)
Shopify loses its position as the preferred commerce platform for independent businesses.
Merchant retention deteriorates significantly.
Enterprise customers begin migrating away.
Merchant Solutions growth slows for several consecutive years.
Free cash flow deteriorates structurally.
Management abandons disciplined capital allocation.
My original investment thesis is no longer valid.

## Known risks
Slower e-commerce growth.
Increased competition from Amazon, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and emerging commerce platforms.
Merchant failures during economic downturns.
Payment pricing pressure.
AI changing how online commerce is built.
International execution risk.
Premium valuation.
